UnZip and install the QStyleSheet folder under MSC

Do not rename the folder or its contents.  These paths 
are referred to in the Q StyleSheet.  The QSS_Dark_Orange sytlesheet
requires the small .png files in the /images folder.

You may (and should) add other Q StyleSheets to the MSC/QStyleSheet/..
folder.  

Select the style sheet from the settings tab in the application.
